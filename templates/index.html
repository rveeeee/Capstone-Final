Your Mental Health Matters

In your journey towards mental well-being, seeking help isn't just important - it's your most empowering choice. Embracing support unlocks pathways to healing, growth, and a life filled with renewed vitality and purpose.

Discover our range of personalized mental health services designed to support your well-being

Counseling

We offer a supportive environment where you can explore your thoughts and feelings, gain insight into your challenges, and develop coping strategies to improve your mental well-being.

Telecounseling

Our telecounseling service provides a convenient and effective alternative to traditional face-to-face counseling, offering confidential support from the comfort and privacy of your own space.

Group Counseling

Our group counseling fosters community, offering shared experiences, mutual support, and new perspectives, enhancing personal growth and emotional well-being in a collaborative, supportive environment.

Frequently Ask Questions

Find answers to common questions about the mental health services available to students.

What mental health services are available to students at the university?

We offer a range of services including individual counseling, group therapy, workshops, and crisis intervention.

How can I make an appointment with a counselor?

You can schedule an appointment by visiting our office, calling our hotline, or using our online booking system available on the university's mental health services webpage.

Are the counseling sessions confidential?

Yes, all counseling sessions are confidential. Information shared during sessions will not be disclosed without your consent, except in cases where there is a risk of harm to yourself or others.

Is there a cost associated with using mental health services?

No, mental health services are provided free of charge to all currently enrolled students.
